## 🎯 Objective

The **Fundamentos de SOC** course provides a comprehensive introduction to the **Security Operations Center (SOC)**, its structure, and key tools for incident investigation and response.  

This course is designed as a starting point for beginners aiming to pursue a career as a SOC Analyst. Participants learn both **technical skills** and a **proactive mindset** for effective cybersecurity operations.

---

## 📝 Course Overview / Index

1. **Introdução ao SOC** – Understanding the purpose and function of a SOC.  
2. **Tipos e funções do SOC** – Explore different types of SOCs and their responsibilities.  
3. **Analista de SOC e suas responsabilidades** – Key tasks and day-to-day activities of a SOC Analyst.  
4. **Relação entre SIEM e Analista** – How Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) tools support SOC operations.  
5. **Gerenciamento de logs** – Best practices for log collection, storage, and analysis.  
6. **EDR — Detecção e Resposta de Pontos Finais** – Understanding Endpoint Detection and Response for incident handling.  
7. **SOAR (Orquestração, Automação e Resposta de Segurança)** – How automation and orchestration improve SOC efficiency.  
8. **Feed de Inteligência de Ameaças** – Using threat intelligence feeds to anticipate and detect attacks.  
9. **Erros comuns cometidos por analistas de SOC** – Common mistakes and how to avoid them.  
10. **Autoavaliação** – Self-assessment to consolidate learning.

These skills are essential for anyone starting a career as a SOC Analyst or pursuing cybersecurity operations.
